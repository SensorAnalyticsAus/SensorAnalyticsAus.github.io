About

Sensor Analytics Australia (SAA) specialises in analyses of IoT data; both static and dynamic types. We provision big data hardware and software platforms for handling small to very large IoT installations. Irrespective of your project size, we should be able to identify an appropriate solution that presents clear ROI. Our initial consults are free. SAA is part of a group with all-in-house capabilities, which enables us to undertake full spectrum implementations - hardware, software, and platform.

SAA is operated by Dr Asad I. Khan who has over 24 years of experience in IT sector as a senior enterprise systems manager, researcher, and university lecturer.
